Ginnie Ooi

Managing Partner, OMNEX

Ginnie Ooi is a seasoned finance and corporate advisory professional with over 20 years of experience spanning accounting, finance, taxation, internal controls, risk management, corporate governance and strategic business advisory. 

She began her career with a leading audit firm and subsequently held senior finance leadership positions in several public-listed companies. Throughout her career, she has developed extensive expertise in corporate finance, treasury management, taxation, regulatory compliance, financial reporting, and governance. She has also played a pivotal role in leading and managing Initial Public Offering (IPO) exercises, corporate restructuring initiatives, and strategic financial transformation projects.

In addition to her executive leadership experience, Ginnie has served as an Independent Non-Executive Director and Board Committee Member for various public-listed companies, providing strategic oversight and governance guidance. Her strong track record in financial leadership, risk management, and corporate governance has enabled organizations to strengthen operational effectiveness, enhance stakeholder value, and achieve sustainable growth.

At OMNEX, Ginnie leads the firm's strategic advisory and financial consulting practice, helping businesses navigate growth, transformation, governance, and performance optimization in an increasingly dynamic business environment.
