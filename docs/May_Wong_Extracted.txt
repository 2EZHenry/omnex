Founder Profile

May Wong is the Founder and Managing Director of OMNEX, a trusted business advisory and outsourcing firm dedicated to helping organizations strengthen their financial foundation, streamline operations, and achieve sustainable growth.

Holding both an MSc and BA (Hons) in Accounting & Finance from the University of Huddersfield, United Kingdom, May combines strong academic credentials with extensive practical experience in accounting, payroll outsourcing, financial reporting, forecasting, budgeting, and business advisory.

With extensive experience across SMEs, startups, and growing enterprises, her expertise lies in transforming financial data into actionable insights, enabling business owners to make informed decisions, improve profitability, manage cash flow effectively, and plan confidently for the future.

Recognizing that businesses require more than traditional accounting support, May established OMNEX to provide integrated financial and operational solutions that empower organizations at every stage of growth. She is passionate about helping business leaders understand their numbers, optimize cash flow, manage risks, and build strategies that drive long-term success through effective forecasting and budgeting.

Under her leadership, OMNEX delivers accounting, payroll management, SST compliance, financial reporting, forecasting, budgeting, and business advisory. Known for her practical, results-oriented approach, May works closely with clients as a strategic business partner, providing reliable financial guidance and operational support.
